Our Story

Back in China, Flora's hometown were filled with beautiful and elegant tea flowers called Chrysanthemum, the flower that symbolizes happiness and vitality. When Flora moved to America for a better opportunity, she missed the fragance and scenary of the "Tea Flower" that filled her hometown. So, she wanted to start a business in America that spreads her cultural differences.

In July 2009, Flora opened Tea Flower Bakery hoping to instill the same symbolism and qualities as the Chrysanthemum Flower in its food. To this day, Tea Flower Bakery sell their food to spread happiness and vitality in every bite while maintaining the elegance and beauty of each carefully hand-crafted bun.
